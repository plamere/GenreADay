About Genre-A-Day

The Echo Nest knows about hundreds and hundreds of genres, from A cappela to Zydeco. With Genre-A-Day you can learn about a new genre, every day for years to come. Every day, a new genre is presented. You can read about the background and history of the genre, listen to its music, learn about the genre's key artists and more.

Genre-A-Day was built using the Echo Nest's Genre APIs. You can learn more about The Echo Nest's extensive music APIs at developer.echonest.com
